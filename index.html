Terence C Langston

Hedge fund manager and founder of Star Asset Managment

Terence Charles Langston (born May 25, 1969) is an American investor, hedge fund manager, and philanthropist. He is the founder and CEO of Pershing Square Capital Management, a hedge fund management company. Langston is considered by some to be a contrarian investor but considers himself an activist investor.
